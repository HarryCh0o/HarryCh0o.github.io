About Me

I am Jiajun Chu, currently pursuing an MPhil degree in Transportation Engineering at the College of Civil Aviation, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, under the supervision of Associate Professor Danwen Bao.

Prior to my MPhil studies, I received bachelor's degrees in Applied Science (Aviation) from RMIT University and in Transportation (Airport Operation and Management) from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.

My research focuses on air transportation systems, operations research, and data-driven decision-making, with particular interests in airport ground operations, flight slot allocation, and UAV integration. I welcome opportunities for academic exchange and collaboration with researchers and students who share similar interests.
